Battle Royale

Create Tournament
Battle Royale tournaments comes in table, and is ranked by the team with the highest win. the tournament host decides if he will rank with first blood or kills if we have two or more teams with same scores. You can create tournament by clicking any of the create tournament buttons.
Join Tournament
To join a Battle Royale tournament, you can go to tournaments page and filter by "BR", select a tournament and click on register button, if the tournament requires more than one players, you can filter and select your teammates before registering.
