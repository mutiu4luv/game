Free

Free tournaments are created without payment, and can be joined without payment too. but the host can attach a token for the winner so you can watch out for that in tournament description

Paid

Paid tournaments are created without payment, but to join you have to pay the specified amount, and the winner leaves with a specified amount too.

Sponsored

Sponsored tournaments are created by making initial payment, you can join without making payment and winner leaves with a specified amount.
